2022-11-22

  • FTOL visualization changed from iTOL to Taxonium. The Taxonium visualization is much faster, allowing for easy viewing directly in the browser.

2022-11-08

  • FTOL v1.2.0 and v1.2.1 released. The total number of species is similar to v1.1.0, but adds > 50 plastome sequences, resulting in a more robust backbone phylogeny.

2022-08-31

  • FTOL featured in 538’s “Data is Plural” blog.

2022-08-24

  • The paper describing FTOL, “An open and continuously updated fern tree of life” published in Frontiers in Plant Science.

2021-07-21

  • Preprint for FTOL available on bioRxiv.

2022-06-24

  • FTO v1.1.0 released. From this point onwards, FTOL releases are tied to specific versions of GenBank.

2022-04-01

  • FTOL v1.0.0 released.
